About Us

Our Mission

The goal of AMEBA is to promote an equitable and inclusive environment that fosters the advancement of PhD students in the Graduate Programs in Bioscience (GPB). Furthermore, we aim to promote diversity in the sciences via outreach to underrepresented communities and youth. AMEBA provides our members with skills and experiences to address these goals.

Programs

Mentorship Program

Connect with experienced professionals to navigate your academic and career journey.

Workshops and Panels

Participate in discussions and activities tailored to bioscience career paths and skills.

Community Engagement

Inspire the next generation of bioscientists through outreach and tutoring programs.
